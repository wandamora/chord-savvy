# **App Name**: ChordSavvy

## Core Features:

- Browse Chord Library: Access and browse a comprehensive library of guitar chord sheets, categorized for easy navigation.
- View Interactive Chord Sheets: Display song lyrics with synchronized chord diagrams and finger placements for easy learning and playing.
- Filter by Known Chords: Filter the song library to show only songs that primarily use chords the user has indicated they already know.
- Song Search: Search for specific songs by title, artist, or genre to quickly find desired chord sheets.
- Personal Favorite Songs: Allow users to save their favorite chord sheets to a personal collection for quick and easy access.
- AI Chord Progression Tool: Utilize an AI tool to suggest harmonious chord progressions based on a selected mood, genre, or key.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: A striking and passionate Red (#FF0000), capturing energy and creativity, used for highlights, interactive elements, and key information.
- Background color: A deep, subtly reddish Charcoal Black (#2B2626), providing a dramatic backdrop that enhances content and reduces eye strain in low-light environments, aligning with the bold aesthetic.
- Accent color: A vibrant fiery Orange (#F77E0E), adding a secondary punch and providing strong visual contrast for calls-to-action and secondary interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, tech-inspired, and slightly angular feel, fitting the digital and creative nature of music.
- Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its high legibility and versatile, neutral design, ensuring chord sheets and song information are easy to read across all devices.
- Use clean, minimalistic icons that are easily recognizable and related to music, instruments, and common interface actions (e.g., search, favorite, filter). Adopt a monochrome or primary color highlight approach for consistency.
- Employ a clean and intuitive layout with ample white space (or in this case, 'black space') to ensure focus on the chord sheet content. Prioritize clear hierarchy and easy navigation to facilitate learning.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth transitions for navigation between songs and filtering results, enhancing user experience without distracting from the core content. Focus on responsive and delightful micro-interactions for buttons and selected states.
